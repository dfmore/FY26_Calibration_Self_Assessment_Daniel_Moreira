Executive Summary

2025 focused on foundational investments in AI-enabled capabilities and infrastructure that will deliver compounding returns. I built the Airtable tracking system serving 16 global activation/onboarding specialists, developed ICM Prophet as a transformative AI vision for hydraulic modelling, and established proactive licensing risk mitigation that has significantly reduced customer disruption.

These foundational efforts—though largely invisible in daily operations—position Customer Success to operate more efficiently in the AI era. Deep AI learning enabled building production tools, establishing frameworks for 2026 vision of integrating multiple data sources to surface knowledge for CS teams.
